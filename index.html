🕐 Analog Clock Kaise Padhein?
🔴
Ghante ki Sui (Hour)
Chotti aur moti sui — yeh ghanta batati hai. Poori ghoom ne mein 12 ghante lagte hain!
🔵
Minute ki Sui (Minute)
Lambi sui — yeh minute batati hai. 1 poori chakkar = 60 minutes = 1 ghanta!
⚡
Second ki Sui
Bahut patli aur tez chalti sui — yeh seconds batati hai. Har second mein ek tick!
🔢
Numbers ka matlab
1–12 tak numbers hain. Minute ke liye: number × 5 = minutes! Jaise 3 matlab 15 minutes.
📍
12 Upar hota hai
12 hamesha sabse upar. Dono suiyan yahan hon toh time hai exactly 12:00!
🌞
AM aur PM
Raat 12 se dopahar 12 tak = AM. Dopahar se raat tak = PM. Subah 6 = 6 AM!
